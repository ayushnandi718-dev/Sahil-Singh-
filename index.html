About the Artist

Sahil Singh is a contemporary Indian singer known for releasing numerous singles, often in Hindi and Bhojpuri. His unique voice has captivated audiences across platforms like JioSaavn, Apple Music, and Spotify.

With popular tracks like "Haee Babuaan Janela Jahan," "Roya Uske Liye," and "Meri Haan," he has established a significant presence. Sahil frequently collaborates with industry names like Big Scratch and Navyug on high-energy tracks like "VELVET".
