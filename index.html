About
Sahil Singh

For Sahil, music is a conversation. Blending traditional soul with modern indie-pop, he creates an unforgettable sonic journey for every listener. His voice carries the weight of stories untold, bridging the gap between melody and pure emotion.

"Every performance is a chance to connect, to share a piece of my world with yours."
